Privacy & Civil Liberties Engineering

Protocol Specification: PCL-2026-REV1

"Infrastructure that records decision-state must be engineered to prevent function creep and unauthorized surveillance."

Structural Decoupling

NADIR artifacts are architecturally isolated from identity-providing systems. By decoupling the inference record from the identity record, institutions can verify the logic of a decision without exposing the personal data of the subject by default.

PCL CONTROL MECHANISMS

  • [01] Ephemeral Capture: Buffering is limited to the sealing window; no long-term persistence of unsealed telemetry.
  • [02] Policy-Based Masking: Sensitive fields are hashed or omitted at the boundary before canonicalization.
  • [03] Non-Reversibility: Deterministic hashes ensure integrity without allowing data reconstruction.

Access Tiers

Disclosure is governed by pre-defined cryptographic tiers. Only authorized keys can unlock full context; public keys only verify the signature.
