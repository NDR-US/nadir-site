Vulnerability Disclosure

NADIR welcomes responsible disclosure of security issues affecting integrity, disclosure controls, or artifact verification workflows.

Read policy Report issue
[SCOPE]

What to report

Issues involving artifact integrity, disclosure boundaries, authentication, authorization, signing workflows, or data exposure risks.

[SUBMISSION]
  • Email: contact@nadir-ai.com
  • Include: affected URL/component, reproduction steps, impact assessment, and any supporting artifacts.
  • Do not include sensitive personal data in reports unless strictly necessary for reproduction.
[SAFE HARBOR INTENT]

NADIR intends to treat good-faith research as constructive. Researchers should avoid disruption, avoid data exfiltration, and coordinate disclosure to prevent harm. This statement is a posture description and may be superseded by written agreements.
