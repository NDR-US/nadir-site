What NADIR is

A compliance overlay that generates deterministic audit artifacts derived from inference-time decision-state representations.

Designed for oversight, incident reconstruction, and verification in high-liability deployments.

What NADIR is not

Not a model. Not a training method. Not a safety policy replacement. Not an “interpretability” claim. NADIR provides integrity and reproducibility of records—your safety and authorization controls remain separate.
