01 · Define Before You Collect

Operators define what fields are included, excluded, and masked before generation. Capture policy is explicit and versioned. Artifacts reflect declared policy; they do not expand beyond it.

02 · Deterministic Normalization

Telemetry is normalized into a deterministic JSON artifact schema (RFC 8785). This produces a stable byte representation independent of serialization order.

03 · Hash & Signature

SHA-256 and ECDSA P-256 sealing ensure tamper-evidence and origin authenticity. The suite selection is configurable based on regulatory needs.
