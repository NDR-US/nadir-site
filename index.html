NADIR: evidentiary infrastructure for AI inference

NADIR generates policy-scoped, canonical records of inference events and seals them with cryptographic integrity controls—so reviewers can later verify provenance and detect tampering without requiring disclosure of proprietary model internals.

Architecture Integration Documents

What it does

Capture (policy-scoped)

Defines what is included, excluded, and masked at the inference boundary.

Canonical record

Normalizes captured fields into a repeatable artifact format under stated conditions.

Integrity + verification

Hashes and signs artifacts so third parties can verify integrity and provenance.

Why it exists

Many systems retain outputs and telemetry, but not a verification-ready record suitable for formal review. NADIR is designed to close that gap with integrity-bound artifacts and a clear disclosure posture.

Boundary Declaration
  • NADIR is record infrastructure, not an adjudicator.
  • It does not certify legality, safety, fairness, or correctness.
  • Oversight conclusions are made by authorized reviewers.
