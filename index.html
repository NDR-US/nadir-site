What CEYO Is

A verification layer for consequential AI operations.

CEYO produces deterministic evidentiary artifacts from policy-scoped inference events.

Those artifacts are designed to support later institutional review, chain-of-custody integrity, and independent verification without default disclosure of model internals, proprietary weights, or full system logic.

It is built for environments where AI-supported actions may require trusted review after the fact.

What CEYO Does Not Claim

Institutional discipline matters.

CEYO is an evidentiary infrastructure. It is not a truth engine, a fairness engine, a compliance certification, a model auditor, or a replacement for institutional judgment.

Verification confirms integrity and provenance under declared policy scope. It does not prove decision correctness, legal sufficiency, or regulatory compliance outcomes.
