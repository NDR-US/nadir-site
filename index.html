Evidentiary Infrastructure

AI systems increasingly influence decisions in institutional environments. When reviewed, infrastructure often produces telemetry not designed to function as durable evidence.

Telemetry describes activity.
It does not preserve proof.

The Accountability Gap

When a decision is questioned, organizations reconstruct events from partial logs. This is interpretive.

CEYO artifacts are canonical, explicit, and independently verifiable.

This is an architectural deficit, not an operational oversight.

Institutional Position

Evidence infrastructure must remain structurally independent from decision authority. CEYO introduces that substrate.

CEYO provides the record.
Institutions provide oversight.
