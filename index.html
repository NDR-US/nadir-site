CEYO is a weight-neutral, policy-scoped evidentiary layer that attaches beside AI inference systems to produce cryptographically sealed canonical artifacts.

[01] THE ACCOUNTABILITY GAP

Systemic Deficit

Modern AI deployments optimize for throughput and latency. Accountability infrastructure is frequently secondary. When a decision is questioned, organizations reconstruct events from partial logs—which is interpretive, not deterministic proof.

Institutions require artifacts that are canonical, explicit in scope, and independently verifiable. This is an architectural deficit, not an operational oversight.

01 / Deterministic + Expand Detail

Canonical Artifacts

Consistent schema and stable byte representation across environments. Canonicalization (RFC 8785) ensures independent recomputation of hashes without interpretive variance.
02 / Cryptographic + Expand Detail

Integrity Sealing

SHA-256 hashing and ECDSA digital signatures establish tamper-evidence and origin authenticity. Once sealed, the decision-state context is frozen for audit.
[02] TECHNICAL PROPERTIES

A Decoupled Substrate

PROPERTY 01 Policy-Scoped Capture
Operators define what fields are included, excluded, and masked before artifact generation begins. Capture policy is explicit and versioned. Artifacts reflect declared policy scope; they do not implicitly expand beyond it. The policy version is recorded within the artifact, making the capture boundary independently reviewable.
PROPERTY 02 Non-Custodial Keys
Signing keys are generated and retained within the client-controlled environment — HSM, KMS, or TEE. CEYO does not possess, transmit, or store private signing keys. This preserves a clear separation between infrastructure provider and cryptographic authority.
PROPERTY 03 Constrained Disclosure
Artifacts may be disclosed under Public / Redacted or Controlled Review tiers. Disclosure tiering allows verification of integrity without default exposure of model weights, proprietary architecture, or confidential system internals.
PROPERTY 04 Fail-Open Architecture
Inference continuity remains independent of artifact sealing. If artifact generation fails, inference proceeds uninterrupted. Failure events are logged and flagged; the artifact is marked as unsealed and the condition becomes reviewable.
BOUNDARY DECLARATION · V2026.02.19

CEYO produces records. It does not validate correctness, fairness, legality, or compliance outcomes. CEYO does not certify regulatory compliance or guarantee legal admissibility.

Authorized institutions and operators retain responsibility for decisions. Verification confirms integrity and provenance under policy scope — not correctness of model output.
