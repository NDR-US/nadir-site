EVIDENTIARY INFRASTRUCTURE

AI systems increasingly influence decisions in regulated, safety-critical, and institutional environments. When those decisions are reviewed, infrastructure often produces telemetry never designed to function as durable evidence.

TELEMETRY DESCRIBES ACTIVITY.
IT DOES NOT PRESERVE PROOF.

CEYO is a decoupled evidentiary layer that attaches beside inference systems to produce policy-scoped, canonical artifacts. These are deterministically structured and cryptographically sealed for verification.

// ARCHITECTURAL_DEFICIT

THE ACCOUNTABILITY GAP

Modern AI deployments optimize for throughput. Accountability is frequently secondary. Reconstruction from logs is interpretive; it is not deterministic.

• EXPLICIT IN CAPTURE SCOPE

• INDEPENDENTLY VERIFIABLE
