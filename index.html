Determinism • Audit Integrity • Controlled Disclosure

Forensic Compliance Artifacts for AI Inference

NADIR sits beside inference and emits structured, tamper-evident artifacts for retention, oversight, and dispute reconstruction. It is designed to support evidentiary review without requiring disclosure of proprietary model weights, source code, or confidential architecture.

What it is

Deterministic capture

Canonicalized records designed for repeatability under defined conditions and policy context.

Tamper-evident integrity

Hashing and verification fields support chain-of-custody workflows suitable for high-liability review.

Constrained disclosure

Verification without disclosing weights, architecture details, or proprietary code paths.

Audit workflow fit

Outputs structured for retention, review, and oversight reporting.

Where it fits

Autonomy & robotics

Route selection, obstacle response, escalation and failover review artifacts.

Decision support

Analyst-assist integrity records for prioritization and escalation pathways.

Post-incident reconstruction

Evidentiary posture for review boards under constrained disclosure requirements.

Non-claims

NADIR does not certify model safety, correctness, or “truth,” and it does not provide interpretability-by-disclosure. It supports evidentiary review by providing reproducible artifacts and tamper-evident integrity suitable for oversight and dispute handling.
