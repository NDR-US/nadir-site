NADIR: deterministic audit artifacts for AI decisions

NADIR attaches at the inference boundary to produce a reproducible, tamper-evident record of decision-state evolution. It is designed to support oversight, incident reconstruction, and verification—without exposing model weights, architecture, or source code.

How it works Briefs & documents

What it is

A compliance overlay that produces deterministic audit artifacts at inference time. It is not a model, not a training method, and not a safety policy replacement.

Deterministic capture

Canonicalized records designed for repeatability under defined conditions.

Tamper-evident integrity

Signatures and verification fields support chain-of-custody workflows.

Constrained disclosure

Verification without disclosing weights, architecture details, or proprietary code paths.

Audit workflow fit

Outputs structured for retention, review, and oversight reporting.

Where it fits

NADIR is designed for mission, regulated, and high-liability deployments where accountability must exist without forcing IP disclosure.

Autonomy & robotics

Route selection, obstacle response, escalation and failover review artifacts.

Decision support

Analyst-assist integrity records for prioritization and escalation pathways.

Post-incident reconstruction

Evidentiary posture for review boards under constrained disclosure requirements.
