Integrity-first records • Controlled disclosure

Evidentiary records for AI inference

NADIR generates policy-scoped, canonical records of inference events and seals them with cryptographic integrity controls—so authorized reviewers can verify provenance and detect tampering without requiring disclosure of proprietary model internals.

How it works Documents Controlled review

Core functions

Capture

Attach beside inference. Scope is policy-defined.

Canonicalize

Normalize and version the record format for repeatability.

Seal

Hash + signature to support authenticity and tamper detection.
