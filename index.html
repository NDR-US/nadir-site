Enter Framework Document Suite

Section A // Structural Deficits

Many AI deployments lack canonical, integrity-bound artifacts suitable for formal review.

01 / Telemetry ≠ Evidence

Surface logs lack deterministic structure and integrity metadata required for admissible review.

02 / Constrained Disclosure

Institutions must verify provenance without default exposure of proprietary model weights.

03 / Liability Ambiguity

Disputes rely on partial telemetry, increasing legal uncertainty for operators and regulators.

Section B // Infrastructure, Not Adjudication

NADIR attaches beside the engine to generate verifiable records.

Policy-Scoped Capture

Operators enumerate included fields and masking rules in a signed Policy Scope.

Canonical Schema

Records are normalized into a deterministic structure including environment fingerprints and hash versions.

Integrity Sealing

Artifacts are canonicalized (RFC 8785) and signed (ECDSA P-256) at the moment of inference.

Controlled Review

Full specifications and verification CLI contracts are available under controlled disclosure.
