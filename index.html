Summary Policy-scoped · Canonicalized · Sealed

CEYO creates audit-ready evidence at the moment an AI system produces a decision output.

  • Operators define exactly what is recorded (policy-scoped capture).
  • Artifacts are canonicalized and sealed so tampering is detectable.
  • Independent reviewers can verify integrity under constrained disclosure.

Record · Seal · Verify

Record

Capture is policy-scoped. Only fields declared in scope are collected. Out-of-scope data is excluded by design.

Seal

Canonicalize deterministically and cryptographically seal the artifact so post-hoc alteration is detectable.

Verify

Independent parties recompute canonicalization and validate signatures to confirm integrity and provenance.
