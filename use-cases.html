Operational integration & use cases

NADIR operates as an overlay at middleware or inference-call boundary layers without modifying core model logic. It does not alter model parameters or access classified datasets.

Technical Architecture Documents

Primary use cases

Designed for environments where accountability must exist without forcing IP disclosure.

Autonomous Navigation Systems

Audit artifacts for route selection, obstacle response, escalation, and failover behavior.

ISR Decision Support Systems

Integrity-anchored artifacts for prioritization, escalation decisions, and analyst-assist workflows.

AI-Enabled Targeting Validation

Deterministic signatures for review, validation gates, and post-event accountability processes.

Incident Reconstruction in Contested (A2/AD) Environments

Chain-of-custody records to support review boards under constrained disclosure requirements.

Integration posture

NADIR is designed to attach at the inference-call boundary (gateway, middleware, or service wrapper). This reduces adoption friction, enables phased deployment, and preserves proprietary implementation boundaries.

Overlay boundary

No core model rewrite required to begin producing audit artifacts.

Minimal exposure

Verification without weight disclosure or architecture exposure.

Audit workflow fit

Artifacts designed for retention, verification, and review under oversight frameworks.

Governance note: NADIR supports integrity and audit readiness. It does not replace safety engineering, policy gating, or human authorization controls in mission-critical environments.
