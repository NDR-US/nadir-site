OVERSIGHT PORTAL

NADIR artifacts are designed to support structured oversight workflows. Verification is mechanical; oversight is institutional.

Regulated Operator

Responsibilities: Capture & Retention

Deploys NADIR alongside inference systems. Configures capture scope, manages signing key custody, and responds to authorized review requests. The operator retains full control over artifact generation.

Authorized Institution

Responsibilities: Validation & Adjudication

Entities with lawful authority to conduct compliance review. They receive artifacts and public-key material to verify integrity. The institution determines evidentiary sufficiency and regulatory implications.

Forensic Auditor

Responsibilities: Independent Verification

Validates artifact integrity and provenance. Auditors document chain-of-custody handling without re-evaluating inference correctness through NADIR infrastructure.

Verification Outcomes

01 / CONFIRMED

Hash and signature validate. No alteration detected since the moment of sealing.

02 / FAILED

Hash mismatch or signature validation failure. Artifact integrity is compromised.

03 / MISMATCH

Policy identifier does not align with expected configuration or capture version.

Oversight Position

NADIR provides infrastructure to support verification; it does not substitute for lawful authority. Oversight conclusions remain the sole responsibility of authorized institutions.
