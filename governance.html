GOVERNANCE PRINCIPLES

Operating Boundaries

Governance is the system that ensures evidence is trustworthy without granting evidence infrastructure control over outcomes. CEYO supports governance through explicit boundaries, versioned policies, and constrained disclosure tiers.

Principle 01
Separation of evidence from authority
CEYO records defined context and seals integrity. Institutions decide whether actions were appropriate.
Principle 02
Pre-declared scope
Collection boundaries must be defined before capture. Artifacts reflect declared policy; they do not expand beyond it.
Principle 03
Verification is mechanical
Verification confirms integrity and provenance under policy scope—not the correctness of model output.
01 / Roles Defined responsibilities
Operator: defines capture policy, key custody, retention, and access controls.
Institution: performs oversight, investigation, audit, or inquiry using sealed artifacts.
CEYO: produces artifacts and verification procedures; does not certify outcomes.
02 / Policy Versioned boundaries
Capture and disclosure rules are versioned and referenced inside each artifact. This enables later reviewers to validate: (a) the declared scope at time of capture, and (b) whether verification was performed against the correct policy version.
03 / Keys Non-custodial authority
Signing keys remain under operator control (HSM/KMS/TEE). CEYO does not possess private keys, which preserves institutional cryptographic authority and reduces conflicts of interest.
04 / Availability Fail-open options
Inference continuity can be maintained independently of sealing. If artifact generation fails, inference proceeds; the event is logged and the artifact is marked unsealed. Deployment posture is operator-defined.
CONSTRAINED DISCLOSURE

Disclosure Tiers

Tier 01
Public
Minimal metadata suitable for general reporting. No proprietary internals. Integrity can still be verified if the tier includes required cryptographic fields.
Tier 02
Redacted
Masked fields per policy (PII, trade secret, sensitive prompts). Intended for internal audit and cross-team investigation where least-privilege access is required.
Tier 03
Controlled Review
Restricted disclosure under explicit authorization, contractual terms, and audit logging. Designed for regulators, incident response, litigation support, and institutional oversight without default weight exposure.
Access & Chain-of-Custody Posture

Access control, retention, and chain-of-custody procedures remain operator governed. CEYO provides a deterministic artifact format and integrity proofs. Institutions establish admissibility posture and procedural handling.
