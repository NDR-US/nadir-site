Governance
Posture

NADIR supports accountable review while preserving confidentiality boundaries. Records are deterministic; institutions render judgment.

Section A // Core Principles

Privacy-by-Design

Capture only policy-defined fields; record Policy ID + version in every artifact to ensure scoped data minimization.

Controlled Disclosure

Tiered visibility allows reviewers to verify integrity without default exposure of trade secrets or proprietary weights.

Auditability

Canonical records supersede ad-hoc logs. Every artifact is reproducible, hash-stable, and suitable for legal review.

Non-Adjudication

NADIR produces the infrastructure for truth; final oversight decisions belong to authorized institutions.

NIST IR 8596 Alignment

Informative Reference Only

Addressing traceability, integrity, and accountability.

NADIR’s design is informed by the NIST IR 8596 “Cyber AI Profile.” This alignment ensures that the evidentiary artifacts generated by our framework meet international expectations for secure AI traceability.
