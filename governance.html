PROTOCOL_01

Minimization by Design

Capture scope is explicitly defined prior to generation. Only declared fields are recorded; unrelated telemetry is excluded by default configuration. Minimization is structural, not aspirational.

PROTOCOL_02

Controlled Disclosure

Artifacts enable verification of integrity without disclosing proprietary architecture, model weights, or source code. Disclosure tiers are defined before artifact generation.

PROTOCOL_03

Deterministic Auditability

Verification is mechanical and reproducible across heterogeneous environments. Interpretation remains an institutional function; integrity remains an architectural one.

PROTOCOL_04

Non-Adjudication

NADIR does not determine legality, fairness, or correctness. It provides integrity-sealed records to the institutions that make those final determinations.

Alignment_Posture

Our architecture reflects principles consistent with contemporary integrity frameworks. We do not claim external endorsement; we provide the infrastructure required to support it.
