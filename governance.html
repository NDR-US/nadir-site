Governance posture

NADIR is designed to support accountable review while minimizing collection and preserving confidentiality boundaries.

Principles

Privacy-by-design

Collect only what is necessary, define scope explicitly, and record policy identifiers and versions.

Controlled disclosure

Enable verification without default exposure of weights, confidential architecture, or proprietary source code.

Auditability

Prefer canonical records designed for formal review over ad-hoc logs.

Non-adjudication

NADIR produces records. Institutions make determinations.

Policies

Public summaries only. Detailed policies can live in controlled documentation.

Human rights policy

NADIR is intended to support accountable review processes consistent with lawful oversight and fundamental rights.
