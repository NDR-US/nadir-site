Verification

Independent Integrity Checks and Declared Review Context

CEYO operates as an independent evidentiary layer positioned alongside AI-supported systems. The platform architecture defines how policy-scoped event data is captured, transformed into artifacts, sealed, and made available for later verification without interfering with the model itself.

Verification focus

External placement, declared capture scope, deterministic transformation, controlled sealing, governance continuity, and later independent review.

Verification Model

Verification asks whether the artifact still means what it claimed to mean at sealing time.

Verification is not a re-run of the originating system. It is an independent evaluation of the artifact against the deterministic rules, integrity state, and provenance context declared when the artifact was sealed.

That means the core verification question is narrow and disciplined: does the artifact remain structurally valid, internally consistent, and attributable to the declared sealing authority?

Independent Review

Verification does not require the artifact reviewer to live inside the originating system.

Verification is designed to occur outside the model and outside the original runtime environment.

That separation matters. It allows an institution, counterparty, or downstream reviewer to evaluate the integrity and provenance of the artifact without requiring direct access to the full internal mechanics of the originating system.

This is why CEYO operates as an independent evidentiary layer rather than as a model-internal monitoring function.

External posture

The reviewer evaluates the artifact from outside the originating system boundary.

No model interference

Verification does not require changing model weights or reopening runtime execution.

Review implications

Later evaluation must be interpreted within the declared capture context.

Lifecycle Governance

Governance becomes durable when every step leaves evidence.

Across discovery, registration, assessment, deployment, and monitoring, CEYO captures policy-permitted artifacts without modifying the originating model or system logic. Each sealing event creates a deterministic, reviewable record that can support later audit, traceability, and institutional verification throughout the lifecycle.

Continuous Governance Loop

CEYO operates as an independent layer across the governance lifecycle, preserving artifacts without interfering with the model itself. That turns governance from a procedural intention into a deterministic record that remains audit-ready across time.

Verification Limits

Verification is narrower than adjudication.

The CEYO platform is designed to generate and preserve evidentiary artifacts.

It is not designed to replace adjudication, determine correctness, evaluate fairness, or resolve regulatory sufficiency.

Its function is narrower and more disciplined: preserve integrity, constrain capture scope, and support later institutional verification.
