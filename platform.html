Integrity metadata

Canonicalization, hashing, signing, timestamps, and custody fields for verifiable integrity proofs.

[CANONICALIZATION]

For JSON artifacts, NADIR uses deterministic canonicalization prior to hashing (RFC 8785) to ensure integrity checks are stable across heterogeneous environments.

[NON-INTERFERENCE POSTURE]

NADIR is designed to operate as a sidecar. Resource isolation mechanisms (e.g., cgroup quotas) are used where applicable to prevent operational back-pressure by design intent.
