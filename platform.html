Architecture

NADIR follows a strict seven-step mechanics chain to produce integrity-sealed artifacts while preserving proprietary boundaries.

01 Attach Boundary 02 Capture Policy Scope 03 Normalize 04 Canonicalize 05 Hash & Sign 06 Enforce Tiers 07 Verify Independent

Stage 1 / Attachment

NADIR deploys as a sidecar, gateway, or wrapper. Instrumentation occurs outside core weights to avoid mutating parameters.

  • • Weight-Neutral: Never ingests model weights by policy.
  • • Resource-Isolation: Enforced cgroup quotas prevent back-pressure.

Stage 3 / Integrity Model

SHA-256 + ECDSA P-256 Default Suite.

Non-Custodial Key Protocol

Signing keys remain inside the operator’s HSM/KMS; NADIR never holds private keys.
