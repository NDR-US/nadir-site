Company · Principles · Controlled disclosure

Neutrality, verifiability, minimal disclosure.

CEYO is evidentiary infrastructure for AI inference systems. It produces integrity-sealed audit artifacts under explicit policy scope, designed for independent verification without default exposure of proprietary internals.

Principles→ Request walkthrough→
Mission

Make verification feasible.

When outcomes are disputed, institutions need records that survive formal review. CEYO focuses on deterministic structure, cryptographic sealing, and verifiable policy boundaries.

Boundary

Records, not judgments.

CEYO does not certify compliance, legality, or decision correctness. Verification confirms integrity and provenance under policy scope.

Principles

The company posture is defined by constraints, not marketing claims.

Neutrality

Evidence infrastructure stays separate from decision authority.

Verifiability

Determinism + sealing enable independent recomputation and PASS/FAIL outcomes.

Minimal disclosure

Tiered disclosure supports review without default exposure of weights or internals.

Operator control

Non-custodial key posture: signing keys remain with the operator (HSM/KMS/TEE).

Evidence infrastructure must remain separate from decision authority. This preserves neutrality and prevents the record layer from becoming an adjudicator of its own output.

CEYO provides the artifact · Institutions provide the oversight
