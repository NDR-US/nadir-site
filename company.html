Company · Principles · Governance posture

Evidence infrastructure for institutional review.

CEYO builds a neutral evidentiary layer for AI inference systems: deterministic capture, canonicalization, and cryptographic sealing to support independent verification under constrained disclosure.

Mission

Close the oversight gap in AI deployments by producing audit-ready records that can survive formal review without default exposure of proprietary model internals.

Position

CEYO produces records. Institutions and operators make determinations. Verification confirms integrity and provenance under policy scope — not correctness of model output.

Principles

Design commitments that shape every deployment and every claim.

NeutralityNon-adjudication

CEYO does not certify compliance, legality, or decision correctness. It provides verifiable records to support oversight processes.

VerifiabilityDeterminism

Canonicalization and sealing enable independent recomputation and PASS/FAIL outcomes under documented procedures.

Minimal disclosureTiered

Disclosure is controlled by policy tiers. Verification is designed to be possible without default exposure of proprietary internals.

Operator controlNon-custodial

Keys remain in operator HSM/KMS/TEE environments. CEYO is designed to operate without possessing private signing keys.

Boundary declaration
  • CEYO does not certify compliance, legality, or decision correctness.
  • CEYO does not guarantee inference reproducibility.
  • CEYO does not guarantee admissibility in any specific proceeding.
  • Verification confirms integrity and provenance under policy scope.

Document set

Public-facing materials remain high-level. Technical and operational detail is available under controlled review.
