Accessibility

NADIR’s public site and any review interfaces are designed with accessibility considerations in mind. This statement describes intent and approach.

Approach

  • Readable typography and high-contrast hierarchy.
  • Keyboard-navigable components and clear focus indicators.
  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility.

Testing posture

Accessibility evaluation may include automated checks and manual review. Where gaps are identified, remediation is prioritized by impact and frequency.

Contact

If you encounter an accessibility issue, contact contact@nadir-ai.com.
