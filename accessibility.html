Accessibility statement

NADIR is committed to providing a digital experience that is accessible to users with disabilities. This statement outlines conformance targets, scope, known limitations, and how to report accessibility issues.

View accessibility scope Report issue
[CONFORMANCE]

Conformance objective

The public NADIR website is designed with reference to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. Conformance is an ongoing objective and may evolve as standards are updated.

[PERCEIVABLE]

Text alternatives for non-text content. Sufficient color contrast between foreground and background.

[OPERABLE]

Keyboard-accessible navigation. Logical focus order. No content that flashes in a way that may cause seizures.

[UNDERSTANDABLE]

Clear navigation labels. Predictable interface behavior. Structured heading hierarchy.

[SCOPE]

Scope and limitations

This statement applies to the public NADIR website. Controlled-access portals and third-party services may have separate accessibility policies.

  • Some downloadable documents (PDF or external formats) may not yet be fully optimized for accessibility.
  • Third-party embedded services are subject to their own accessibility practices.
  • Accessibility features are tested periodically but may not cover every assistive technology combination.
[FEEDBACK]

Reporting and review

Accessibility feedback is reviewed as part of ongoing site maintenance.

To report an accessibility issue, email contact@nadir-ai.com. Please include the page URL, a description of the issue, and any assistive technology used.

[UPDATE CADENCE]

This accessibility statement is reviewed periodically and may be updated to reflect changes in standards or implementation.
