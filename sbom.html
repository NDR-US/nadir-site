Software bill of materials

NADIR supports Software Bill of Materials (SBOM) publication suitable for institutional procurement workflows. Where operational security or client confidentiality requires constrained disclosure, SBOM access may be provided under controlled review.

SBOM posture Request controlled review
[DELIVERABLE]

SBOM deliverable scope

SBOMs are intended to support supply chain risk review and dependency transparency. This page describes NADIR’s publication posture; it does not disclose internal build details on the public surface.

[FORMAT]

Standard formats

SBOMs may be provided in recognized machine-readable formats such as SPDX and CycloneDX, depending on integration and procurement requirements.

[INTEGRITY]

Integrity controls

SBOM artifacts may be versioned and integrity-sealed (hash + signature) to support authenticity validation in review workflows.

[BOUNDARIES]

Constrained disclosure

Public summaries remain high-level. Full dependency lists and build manifests may be shared under controlled review when appropriate.

[NOTE]

Procurement reviewers may request: SBOM format, signing method, version mapping, update cadence, and contact for vulnerability coordination.

[GENERATION]

Generation and update cadence

NADIR SBOMs are designed to be generated from build pipelines and release artifacts. Update cadence is tied to release cycles and may be adapted for controlled environments.

[RELEASE]

Release mapping

SBOM versions map to product releases and may include a build identifier for traceability in institutional review.

[PATCH]

Patch posture

Security patches may trigger SBOM regeneration and re-issuance to ensure supply chain transparency remains current.

[CONTROL]

Controlled environments

In restricted deployments, SBOM distribution may be limited to authorized recipients under procurement or security review.

[BOUNDARY]

Disclosure boundaries

SBOM transparency supports supply chain review. It does not imply certification, endorsement, or warranty, and it does not grant any rights to proprietary materials.

[STATEMENT]
  • Public SBOM posture is descriptive only and may remain redacted.
  • Full SBOM artifacts may be provided under controlled review.
  • SBOM disclosure does not grant a license to NADIR intellectual property.
  • Security findings should be reported through the VDP channel.
[SECURITY]

Report vulnerabilities to security@nadir-ai.com. Coordinated disclosure is described on the VDP page.
