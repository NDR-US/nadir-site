Accountability
is Physical.

Ethical AI is a function of transparency and durability. NADIR provides the mechanical foundation for responsibility by ensuring that the record cannot be retroactively altered.

[ 01. Honest Records ]

Integrity Above All

We believe the first ethical duty of an infrastructure provider is the truth of the record. NADIR prevents the sanitization of "uncomfortable" inference data.

[ 02. Institutional Agency ]

Anti-Adjudication

It is unethical for software to replace human judgment in legal or moral contexts. NADIR empowers humans to judge by providing proof; it does not judge for them.

[ 03. Functional Scoping ]

Intentional Limits

A tool that can record everything is a tool that can be weaponized. We limit our capture capabilities to the inference boundary to protect individual liberty.

Ethical Boundary Declaration

"NADIR does not promise 'Good' AI. We promise a record durable enough to determine if the AI was 'Bad'."
