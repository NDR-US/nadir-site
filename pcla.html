Privacy & Civil Liberties Assessment (PCLA)

Institutional Template for Deployer Compliance

This template is provided to assist institutions in evaluating the PCL impact of NADIR evidentiary layers. It focuses on the structural decoupling of inference records from subject identity.

Section 01: Purpose Limitation

Are NADIR artifacts restricted to the verification of system decision-state?

Verification: Artifacts are policy-scoped and cryptographically bound to the inference event. They cannot be repurposed for identity tracking.

Section 02: Data Minimization

Does the capture policy exclude personally identifiable information (PII) by default?

Verification: Capture is limited to input/output features declared in the policy manifest. Redaction occurs at the boundary.

Section 03: Access Control & Disclosure

Is disclosure tiered based on institutional authority?

Verification: Cryptographic sealing allows for "Integrity Checks" without "Content Disclosure."
