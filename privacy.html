Privacy statement

NADIR is designed to minimize collection by default. Capture scope is explicitly defined by policy and aligned to lawful oversight requirements. This public page is a high-level summary only.

Read summary PCL engineering
[SUMMARY]

Policy-scoped and bounded

Operators determine what is collected, excluded, and masked. Disclosure boundaries are configurable per deployment and audience.

[ROLE]

Controller and processor posture

In typical deployments, operators act as data controllers and NADIR operates as a data processor within the scope of defined capture policy. Actual legal roles depend on deployment structure and jurisdiction.

[RETENTION]

Retention is operator-defined

Retention periods are not prescribed by NADIR. Operators define retention posture consistent with applicable law and institutional requirements.

[DISCLOSURE]

Tiered visibility

Disclosure tiers allow integrity verification without default exposure of sensitive fields, proprietary internals, or unnecessary personal data.

[NOTICE]

This public page is a high-level summary only. Detailed privacy posture belongs in controlled operational documentation and deployment-specific policy.
