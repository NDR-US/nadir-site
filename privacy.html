Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

1.0 Scope of Infrastructure

1.1

NADIR is a provider of evidentiary infrastructure. This policy governs our data handling practices as they relate to the deployment of our integrity substrate. Note that NADIR predominantly operates in a "non-custodial" capacity.

2.0 Data Minimization by Design

2.1

Boundary Ingestion: Our systems are engineered to ingest only the specific inference-state variables defined by the User's capture policy. We do not ingest model weights or comprehensive training data.

2.2

Non-Persistence: Unless otherwise configured by the Operator, raw telemetry is processed ephemerally for the purpose of canonicalization and sealing. Only the sealed artifact persists.

3.0 Third-Party Access

3.1

We do not sell, rent, or trade inference-state artifacts. Access to artifacts is controlled exclusively by the deploying institution via their private cryptographic keys.
