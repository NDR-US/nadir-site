POLICY-SCOPED COLLECTION • TIERED DISCLOSURE • MINIMAL RETENTION

Privacy statement

NADIR is designed to minimize collection by default. Capture scope is explicitly defined by policy and aligned to lawful oversight requirements.

Summary

NADIR supports policy-scoped records and tiered disclosure. Operators determine what is collected, what is excluded, and what is masked. Operators act as data controllers for purposes of applicable privacy law. NADIR operates as a data processor within the scope of defined capture policy.

Retention periods are not prescribed by NADIR. Operators are responsible for defining retention posture consistent with applicable legal and institutional requirements.

This public page is a high-level summary only. Detailed privacy posture belongs in controlled operational documentation.
