Privacy statement

NADIR is designed to minimize collection by default. Capture scope should be explicitly defined by policy and aligned to lawful oversight requirements.

Summary

NADIR supports policy-scoped records and tiered disclosure. Operators determine what is collected, what is excluded, and what is masked.

This public page is a high-level summary only. Detailed privacy posture belongs in controlled operational documentation.
