Privacy statement

1. Purpose

This statement describes how the CEYO public website handles information.

2. Information collected

Contact submissions: name, email, message content, and any information you choose to provide.

Basic technical logs: standard server logs may include IP address, user agent, and request metadata for security and reliability.

3. How information is used

Respond to inquiries and controlled review requests. Maintain security and prevent abuse.

4. Sharing

CEYO does not sell personal information. CEYO may share information with service providers strictly to operate the site and communications, subject to confidentiality obligations, or as required by law.

5. Data retention

Contact submissions are retained as necessary to respond and maintain business records. Security logs are retained for a limited period consistent with security operations needs.

6. Security

Do not submit sensitive personal data or classified information through the public contact form.

7. Updates

This statement is versioned and may be updated. The current version appears in the header.
