Privacy

Privacy statement

This statement describes how CEYO handles information on the public website and in communications. CEYO is record infrastructure. Deployment data is operator-controlled and governed by the operator’s policy, systems, and law.

Summary: The public website is designed to minimize collection. Do not submit sensitive personal or confidential operational data through the contact form.

Public site data

What we may collect
  • Basic logs necessary to operate and secure the site (e.g., request metadata, error logs).
  • Information you choose to submit via contact (name, email, organization, message).
What we do not seek
  • Sensitive personal information.
  • Operational secrets, credentials, or proprietary payloads.
  • Customer datasets or inference inputs/outputs.

Contact submissions

Purpose
  • Respond to inquiries and coordinate evaluation or controlled review.
  • Maintain basic correspondence records for continuity and security.

Deployments

CEYO deployments are operated by the deploying organization. Capture scope is policy-defined and can be configured to exclude or mask fields. Operators are responsible for lawful collection, privacy compliance, and retention controls.

Boundary
  • CEYO does not require custody of private signing keys.
  • CEYO does not determine legality or correctness of outcomes.
  • Artifact sufficiency for any proceeding is authority-determined.

Updates

This statement may be revised. The current version is the one published on this page.
