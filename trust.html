Trust Center

Verifiable Mechanics

Trust in evidentiary systems is established through reproducible integrity mechanics — determinism, non-custodial cryptographic authority, and independent verification — with explicit boundaries around what is and is not claimed.

[01] Integrity Architecture

Deterministic Artifacts

Artifacts are generated through deterministic canonicalization (RFC 8785), cryptographic hashing, and digital signatures. Verification is reproducible across environments and does not depend on proprietary internals.

Determinism · Tamper-evidence · Origin authenticity
[02] Key Custody

Non-Custodial Signing

Signing keys remain within the operator-controlled environment (HSM, KMS, TEE). CEYO does not possess, transmit, or store private signing keys. Authenticity remains anchored to the deploying institution.

Operator authority · Clear liability boundaries
[03] Verification

Independent Procedure

Verification consists of deterministic canonicalization, hash recomputation, signature validation, and policy alignment checks. Outcomes are explicit: Integrity Confirmed, Integrity Failed, Policy Scope Mismatch.

Mechanical verification · Institutional interpretation
[04] Supply Chain Transparency

SBOM Posture

Reference builds maintain an SBOM in standard formats (SPDX, CycloneDX) for controlled review. Deployment-specific SBOM varies by operator configuration and environment.

Supply chain review support
[05] Vulnerability Disclosure

VDP / Safe Harbor

A structured Vulnerability Disclosure Program supports good-faith reporting within defined boundaries. Production deployments follow contractual security terms.

Disclosure channel · Coordinated remediation
[06] Sustainability

Compute-to-Integrity

Artifact generation introduces incremental workload: capture, canonicalization, hashing, signatures. Impact depends on capture scope, cryptographic suite, and infrastructure selected by the operator.

No universal energy claims
[07] Privacy & Civil Liberties

Minimization by Design

Policy-scoped capture prevents uncontrolled surveillance expansion. Fields are included, excluded, and masked before generation; tiered disclosure boundaries are explicit.

Purpose limitation · Explicit scope boundaries
[08] Legal Context

Admissibility Primer

Integrity-sealed artifacts may support authentication and reliability arguments depending on jurisdiction and institutional handling. CEYO does not guarantee admissibility.

Informational only · Not legal advice
