Modern slavery & human rights statement

NADIR is committed to respecting internationally recognized human rights and to opposing modern slavery, forced labor, and human trafficking in its operations and supply relationships. This statement describes scope, boundaries, and review posture.

View scope Report concern
[SCOPE]

Operational scope

This statement applies to NADIR’s public-facing operations and direct supplier relationships. It does not automatically extend to independent client environments or third-party services.

  • Internal business operations and governance.
  • Direct service providers and contracted vendors.
  • Infrastructure providers engaged for hosting or tooling.
[DUE DILIGENCE]

Supplier expectations

Suppliers are expected to operate in accordance with applicable labor laws and human rights standards. This expectation may be reflected in contractual terms where appropriate.

[FORCED LABOR]

No tolerance for forced, bonded, or involuntary labor.

[CHILD LABOR]

Compliance with applicable minimum age laws.

[FAIR TREATMENT]

Respect for lawful working conditions and non-discrimination.

[REPORTING]

Reporting concerns

Concerns relating to modern slavery or human rights may be reported confidentially.

To report a concern, email contact@nadir-ai.com. Include relevant details to allow review.

[REVIEW CADENCE]

This statement is reviewed periodically and may be updated to reflect operational or regulatory developments.
