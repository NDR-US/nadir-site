Ethical use

NADIR is designed for deployment by authorized institutions in lawful oversight contexts. Use of NADIR infrastructure for purposes inconsistent with lawful oversight, human rights obligations, or applicable law is not authorized.

[POLICY]

Authorized use in oversight contexts

Ethical posture is enforced through disclosure boundaries, policy-scoped capture, and operator responsibility.

Authorized deployment contexts

NADIR is intended for regulated operators, institutional oversight bodies, and organizations with lawful authority to conduct structured review of AI inference systems.

Operator responsibility

Operators are responsible for ensuring deployment and use of NADIR infrastructure complies with applicable law, institutional policy, and ethical obligations in their jurisdiction and domain.

Non-weaponization

NADIR infrastructure must not be used to circumvent legal protections, enable unauthorized surveillance, or produce artifacts intended to misrepresent inference behavior. Nothing in this policy constitutes authorization for military, intelligence, or enforcement activity absent lawful authority and compliance with applicable law.

Human oversight

NADIR supports human oversight workflows. It is not a substitute for human judgment, legal process, or institutional accountability mechanisms.

[BOUNDARY]
  • NADIR does not authorize any use that violates applicable law or international humanitarian law.
  • NADIR does not endorse, certify, or validate the lawfulness of any AI system or its outputs.
  • Deployment responsibility and ethical accountability remain with the implementing organization.
