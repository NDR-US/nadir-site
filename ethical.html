Ethical Use
Policy

NADIR is infrastructure for lawful oversight—not a tool for clandestine surveillance or disinformation.

Authorized Deployments

  • • Regulated operators (Finance, Health)
  • • Government oversight bodies
  • • Independent auditors

Operator Responsibilities

  • — Define explicit capture policy and disclosure tiers
  • — Retain artifacts per applicable jurisdictional law
  • — Ensure mandatory human review of sensitive outputs

Red-Line Prohibitions

  • ✗ Untargeted biometric scraping
  • ✗ Unauthorized mass surveillance
  • ✗ Fabrication of disinformation artifacts
  • ✗ Lethal autonomous loops (No Human Override)

Human-in-the-Loop

NADIR augments—not replaces—human judgment. Every critical decision must include a human review of the artifact trail.
