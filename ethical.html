AUTHORIZED USE · OVERSIGHT CONTEXTS · OPERATOR RESPONSIBILITY

Ethical use

NADIR is intended for lawful oversight contexts. Use of NADIR infrastructure for purposes inconsistent with lawful oversight, human rights obligations, or applicable law is not authorized.

Intended use

Authorized deployment contexts

NADIR is intended for use by regulated operators, institutional oversight bodies, and organizations with lawful authority to conduct structured review of AI inference systems.

Operator responsibility

Operators are responsible for ensuring their deployment and use of NADIR infrastructure complies with applicable law, institutional policy, and ethical obligations in their jurisdiction and domain.

Non-weaponization

NADIR infrastructure must not be used to circumvent legal protections, enable unauthorized surveillance, or produce artifacts intended to misrepresent inference behavior. Nothing in this document constitutes authorization for military, intelligence, or enforcement activity absent lawful authority and compliance with applicable law.

Human oversight

NADIR supports human oversight workflows. It is not a substitute for human judgment, legal process, or institutional accountability mechanisms.

Boundary declaration
  • NADIR does not authorize any use that violates applicable law or international humanitarian law.
  • NADIR does not endorse, certify, or validate the lawfulness of any AI system or its outputs.
  • Deployment responsibility and ethical accountability remain with the implementing organization.
