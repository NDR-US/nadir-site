Verify · Integrity · Provenance

Verify an artifact.

Paste a CEYO sealed envelope below. The verifier runs entirely in your browser — no data leaves your machine. Provide a public key to validate the ECDSA signature; omit it to check hash integrity only.

What this checks
  • Canonicalization — reproduces the RFC 8785 (JCS) canonical form of the artifact body.
  • Hash integrity — recomputes SHA-256 and compares it to the stored value.
  • Signature — validates the ECDSA P-256 signature over the canonical body using your provided public key.
