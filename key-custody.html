Key Custody Declaration

NADIR is designed so signing keys are generated and retained within the client-controlled environment. NADIR does not possess private keys by default.

[STATEMENT]

Custody and control

This declaration exists to clarify liability boundaries and procurement expectations regarding signing keys and evidence integrity.

[DEFAULT POSTURE]
  • Keys are generated and retained within the client-controlled environment (HSM/KMS where available).
  • NADIR does not possess or transmit private keys by default.
  • Custody models are documented per deployment and may be reflected in audit packets.
[BOUNDARY]

This page describes NADIR’s architectural intent and standard operating posture. Specific customer arrangements may require separate written agreements and deployment-specific documentation.
