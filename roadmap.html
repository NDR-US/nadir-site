PROOF → VERIFICATION → WORKFLOW → EXPANSION

Roadmap

The product is proven records. NADIR prioritizes canonical artifacts, verification, and chain-of-custody before expanding scope.

Current position

Phase 1 — Delivery of Stages 1–3 (Capture, Canonicalization, Sealing).

Interpretation
Stages are the mechanics chain steps in the data pipeline. Phases are delivery milestones over time.

PHASE 1 — CORE MECHANICS

Delivery of Stages 1–3.

Stage 1: Capture
Stage 2: Canonicalization (masking pre-hash)
Stage 3: Sealing (hash + signature)

PHASE 2 — VERIFICATION

Verification harness and institutional review readiness.

Recompute hashes, validate signatures, confirm policy alignment under defined conditions.

PHASE 3 — CHAIN-OF-CUSTODY WORKFLOW

Retention posture and controlled review outputs.

Storage, retrieval, audit-ready record handling, and constrained disclosure reporting.
QUALITY BAR
  • Expand only after determinism and verification are proven in constrained scope.
  • Keep claims limited to integrity, provenance, and workflow support.
  • Oversight conclusions belong to authorized reviewers.
