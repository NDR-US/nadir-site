Milestones • Validation • Readiness

Roadmap

A phased path focused on reproducibility, integrity, and auditor-verifiable artifacts—prior to broad deployment.

Alpha

Determinism baseline

Establish repeatable artifact generation under controlled conditions. Produce a reference evidence package format.

Beta

Non-invasive capture

Integrate capture into common inference patterns with minimal operational disruption. Add disclosure profiles and retention controls.

Pilot

Forensic replay workflow

Demonstrate dispute handling: verify integrity, reconstruct events, and produce review board artifacts end-to-end.

Deployment

Operational hardening

Access controls, monitoring, incident flags, audit sampling, and policy-driven capture scope for high-volume systems.

Evaluation posture

NADIR is designed for controlled evaluation and staged validation. Public claims remain intentionally bounded to avoid over-promising.
