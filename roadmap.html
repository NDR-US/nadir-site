[PHASED EXECUTION]

Scope discipline

Each phase expands only after determinism and verification are proven in constrained scope.

[PHASE 1]

Canonical artifact & prototype sealing

Define policy-scoped capture. Normalize deterministically. Canonicalize prior to hashing (RFC 8785 for JSON artifacts). Hash and sign. Enforce fail-open logging posture.

[PHASE 2]

Verification harness

Independent verification tooling: hash recomputation, signature validation, and policy alignment checks under controlled disclosure.

[PHASE 3]

Chain-of-custody workflow

Retention posture, retrieval logging, review-ready outputs, and handling procedures suitable for formal proceedings.
