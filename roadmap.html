PROOF → VERIFICATION → WORKFLOW → EXPANSION

Roadmap

The product is proven records. NADIR prioritizes canonical artifacts, verification, and chain-of-custody before expanding scope.

Current position: Phase 1 — Canonical Artifact Definition & Prototype Sealing.

Phase 1 — Canonical artifact

Define capture policy

What is included, excluded, and masked. Version the policy.

Canonicalize

Normalize ordering and serialization so verification is repeatable under defined capture conditions.

Phase 2 — Integrity + verification

Seal records

Hash and sign the canonical record. Define key custody.

Verification harness

Recompute and validate integrity fields under defined conditions.

Phase 3 — Chain-of-custody workflow

Retention posture

Storage, retrieval, and audit-ready record handling.

Review output

Controlled reports that preserve confidentiality while enabling verification.

Quality bar
  • Expand only after determinism and verification are proven in constrained scope.
  • Keep claims limited to integrity, provenance, and workflow support.
  • Oversight conclusions belong to authorized reviewers.
