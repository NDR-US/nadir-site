PROOF → VERIFICATION → WORKFLOW → EXPANSION

Roadmap

The product is proven records. NADIR prioritizes canonical artifacts, verification, and chain-of-custody before expanding scope.

Current position

Phase 1 — Delivery of Stages 1–3 (Capture, Canonicalization, Sealing). Claims remain limited to integrity, provenance, and workflow support.

PHASE 1 — CORE MECHANICS

Delivery of Stages 1–3 (Capture, Canonicalization, Sealing). Define capture policy, normalize deterministically, hash and sign.

PHASE 2 — VERIFICATION

Verification harness and reviewer workflow. Recompute, validate signature, confirm policy alignment under controlled disclosure.

PHASE 3 — CHAIN-OF-CUSTODY WORKFLOW

Retention posture, retrieval logging, audit-ready handling, and controlled review outputs suitable for formal proceedings.

Quality Bar
  • Expand only after determinism and verification are proven in constrained scope.
  • Keep claims limited to integrity, provenance, and workflow support.
  • Oversight conclusions belong to authorized reviewers.
