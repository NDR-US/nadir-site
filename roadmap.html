Roadmap

NADIR prioritizes canonical artifacts, verification, and chain-of-custody before expanding scope. Claims remain limited to integrity and workflow support.

Current position

Phase 1 — Canonical Artifact & Prototype Sealing.

Phase 1 · Current

Canonical artifact & prototype sealing. Deterministic normalization. Prototype sealing. Policy configuration model.

Phase 2

Verification harness. Independent verification tooling. Signature validation workflow. Policy alignment enforcement.

Phase 3

Chain-of-custody workflow. Retrieval logging. Retention posture. Review-ready outputs suitable for formal proceedings.

Quality bar
  • Expand only after determinism and verification are proven in constrained scope.
  • Keep claims limited to integrity, provenance, and workflow support.
  • Oversight conclusions belong to authorized reviewers.
