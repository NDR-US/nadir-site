Founder’s letter

NADIR exists to make review possible when AI systems matter — without requiring disclosure of proprietary internals to prove integrity.

AI systems are increasingly present in environments where the cost of ambiguity is unacceptable. Yet most deployments preserve outputs and surface telemetry, not canonical records designed to survive formal review.

NADIR is built around a simple idea: when decisions are disputed later, institutions need integrity-sealed records with defined boundaries. NADIR does not claim correctness. It produces records that can be verified.

The objective is clarity under constrained disclosure: protect proprietary model weights and confidential architecture while enabling independent verification of provenance and tamper-resistance.

It produces records capable of deterministic verification.

— Brian Covarrubias
NADIR
