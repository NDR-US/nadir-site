Founder’s
Statement

When accountability matters, evidence must be verifiable. NADIR exists to notarize decision state without forcing innovators to reveal their intellectual property.

I have spent fifteen years at the intersection of machine learning R&D and regulated infrastructure. The pattern is always the same: an engineering team optimizes for throughput; a compliance team asks for proof.

The logs are incomplete, the context is missing, and everyone discovers—too late—that telemetry is not evidence.

Property 01

Canonical

— Identical everywhere it is verified.

Property 02

Tamper-Evident

— A single-bit change must fail validation.

Property 03

Scoped

— Revealing only what policy demands, nothing more.

NADIR implements those three properties via RFC 8785 canonicalization, SHA-256 / ECDSA-P256 tamper evidence, and tiered disclosure.

We are not an ethics engine, a bias filter, or a policy oracle. We are a digital notary for AI inference. Our job is to seal the record so auditors, regulators, and courts can do theirs.

Brian Covarrubias
Founder, NADIR
