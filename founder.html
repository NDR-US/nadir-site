Why NADIR exists

Infrastructure for accountability must exist before accountability can be exercised.

Artificial intelligence systems are increasingly embedded in decision-making environments that affect finance, healthcare, public administration, and defense. Yet the evidentiary layer required to examine how those systems behave is often absent. Decisions are produced. Outputs are logged. But structured, verifiable artifacts that can withstand institutional scrutiny are rarely generated by default.

NADIR exists to address that structural deficit.

It is not a model. It is not a bias detector. It is not an adjudication engine. It is an evidentiary infrastructure layer designed to produce cryptographically sealed records of defined interaction boundaries. Institutions remain responsible for interpretation, governance, and determination.

The core principle is simple: accountability requires artifacts. Artifacts require integrity. Integrity requires separation of concerns. By decoupling verification from inference, NADIR aims to make record generation durable without interfering with operational systems.

From the beginning, the posture has been deliberate:

  • • Non-custodial signing architectures where feasible.
  • • Deterministic canonicalization prior to hashing.
  • • Cryptographic agility as standards evolve.
  • • Explicit boundaries on what the system does not do.

NADIR does not certify compliance. It does not render legal conclusions. It does not determine truth. It provides structured, integrity-sealed artifacts that authorized institutions may evaluate under their own statutory or policy frameworks.

The goal is not visibility for its own sake. The goal is institutional durability. Systems change. Models update. Policies evolve. Records must remain verifiable across those transitions.

This project was built from the recognition that governance cannot operate on memory or screenshots. It requires reproducible evidence.

NADIR is an infrastructure attempt to make that evidence structurally possible.

Brian Covarrubias
Founder, NADIR
